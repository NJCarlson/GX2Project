Old Class project for Full Sail Computer Graphics 2 class. Basic example app of rendering custom graphics.

SPOTLIGHT CONTROLS : 
The 'L' Key snaps the spotlight position back to its original place.

following keys are on the keypad:
*NUMLOCK MUST BE ON*
8 and 5 : move spotlight forward and backward in the Z direction respectively

4 and 6: move the spotlight forward and backward in the x direction respectively

7 and 9: increases and decreases the z component of the cone angle respectively
